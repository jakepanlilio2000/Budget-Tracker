# Support Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to ExpensePro! We are committed to providing a robust, enterprise-grade financial management platform. If you encounter issues, have questions, or need guidance on using the application, this document outlines the best ways to get help.

## Before Requesting Support
To ensure you get the fastest and most accurate assistance, please take a moment to:
1. **Read the Documentation:** Thoroughly review the [README.md](README.md) for installation, configuration, and usage instructions.
2. **Check the FAQ:** Look through the [FAQ section](README.md#faq) in the README for answers to common questions.
3. **Search Existing Issues:** Search the [GitHub Issues tracker](https://github.com/yourusername/expensepro/issues) to see if your question or bug has already been reported and resolved.
4. **Verify Your Version:** Ensure you are running the latest stable release of ExpensePro, as your issue may have already been patched.

## Getting Help
If you still need assistance, you can reach out to the community and maintainers through the following channels:
- **GitHub Discussions:** (Recommended) For general questions, architectural advice, or community chat.
- **GitHub Issues:** For specific bug reports or feature requests.

## Bug Reports
If you have found a bug, please open a new issue using the **Bug Report** template. To help us investigate and resolve the issue quickly, please include:
- **Environment Details:** Your PHP version, MySQL/MariaDB version, Operating System, and Web Server (e.g., Nginx/Apache).
- **Browser Details:** Browser name and version (if the issue is UI-related).
- **Steps to Reproduce:** Clear, step-by-step instructions on how to trigger the bug.
- **Expected vs. Actual Behavior:** What you expected to happen and what actually happened.
- **Screenshots/Logs:** Relevant screenshots, error messages, or entries from `storage/logs/`.

## Feature Requests
We welcome ideas to improve ExpensePro! When suggesting a feature, please use the **Feature Request** template and include:
- **The Problem:** What challenge are you trying to solve?
- **Proposed Solution:** How do you envision this feature working?
- **Use Case:** How will this benefit the broader user base?
- **Alternatives Considered:** Have you looked at existing features that might already solve this?

## Security Issues
**Do not** report security vulnerabilities via public GitHub issues. 
Please follow the responsible disclosure process outlined in our [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) document.

## Community Guidelines
We expect all participants in our support channels to adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Be respectful, constructive, and patient. Remember that maintainers and contributors are often volunteering their time.

## Response Expectations
ExpensePro is maintained by a dedicated team of contributors. While we strive to respond to all issues promptly, response times may vary depending on project availability, issue complexity, and community contributions. Thank you for your patience and understanding.
